About CCST

Committed to Excellence in Education
It was develop to support students studying in private schools
with comprehensive resources, cozy classrooms and very affordable tuition costs.
CCST was established in order to provide the information and instructions
required to increase the standards of education provided in the province of pampanga.
The institution was registered on March 17, 2005, and was
formally established on June 20 of the same year.

Who We Are

Clark College of Science and Technology (CCST) is a leading institution dedicated to providing high-quality education in both academic and technical fields. Since its establishment, CCST has been shaping students into competent and globally competitive professionals.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and relevant academic and technical programs that empower students with knowledge, skills, and values needed for lifelong success.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in education, producing graduates who contribute positively to society and global development.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Discipline, and Social Responsibility guide everything we do at CCST.
